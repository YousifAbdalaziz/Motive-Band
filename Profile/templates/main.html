About Us

Welcome to the official website of Motive Band! We are a group of Sudanese youth who believe that music is a universal language that unites hearts, Through our art, we seek to deliver a message of hope and positivity, drawing inspiration from our rich culture.

We are an energetic musical group dedicated to bringing you the best live performances and original music. Our sound blends various genres, creating a unique experience for our fans.
